Problem Statement :

* KPI's Requirement
We need to analyze key indicators for our pizza sales data to gain insights into our business performance metrics, we want to calculate the following metrics :

1.Total Revenue : The sum of all pizza orders.
2.Average order value : The average amount spent per order, calculated
by dividing the total revenue by total number of orders.
3.Total Pizza Solds: The sum of Quantity of all pizza.
4.Total Orders : The total number of order placed.
5 Average pizza per order : The average pizza sold per order, calculated by dividing the total number of pizzas sold by the total numbers of orders.

2.Monthly trends
3.Percentage of sales by Pizza Category.
4.Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size.
5.Total Pizza sold by pizza Category.
6.Top 5 Best Sellers by Revenue.
7.Bottom 5 Best/Worst sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity and Total orders.
